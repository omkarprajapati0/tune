Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play a song?

Find your fvt Song and click on the track you want. It will start playing instantly in the player at the bottom.

How do I find songs categories?

Go to the Homepage, click “ ☰ ”, You will find Song Categories.

Why can’t I see lyrics?

Lyrics are available only for certain songs. If lyrics exist, you’ll see a “Lyrics” option in the Now Playing sidebar.

How do I change my account details?

Visit the Account page (under Settings) to update your username, email, or password.

Can I use TuneWave offline?

Currently, TuneWave requires an internet connection. Offline playback is a feature we’re exploring for future updates.
