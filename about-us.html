About TuneWave

Your personal music hub — listen, explore, enjoy.

Our Mission

At TuneWave , our goal is to bring you a seamless and intuitive music experience. Whether you're exploring new tracks or revisiting your favorites, we aim to make music discovery fun and easy.

What We Offer

• A curated library of songs, albums, artists, and genres.
• Recently played & Now Playing feature so you never lose track of what you're listening to.
• Lyrics view (where available) to sing along.
• A clean, minimal UI designed for quick navigation and ease of use.
